生け花/活花 いけばな

3D visualization of modern ikebana that combines the organic forms of succulents with minimalist stone. The project explores the harmony between plant life and stone stillness in digital space

Glimpses into a fantastical ecosystem, these designs showcase speculative plant life. With pearlescent sheens, translucent bodies, and soft, glowing colours, they evoke the beauty of alien botany or lifeforms from a dream

some scatches

This arrangement celebrates nature as the ultimate muse. Imagine a hidden glade where water seeps from moss-covered rock, nurturing fragile beauty. The stone provides anchor, the water lends life, and the flowers reach for light – a quiet testament to nature's artistry

This Ikebana arrangement draws inspiration directly from the earth. A moss-covered stone forms the foundation, mimicking a natural landscape element from which life-giving water gently emerges. Delicate flowers rise from this source, symbolizing growth and beauty sustained by nature itself A mossy stone, whispering of ancient earth, gives rise to both a gentle water source and delicate blossoms. This Ikebana piece captures the essence of natural creation, where water and life spring forth from the grounded beauty of the wild
