Try your fortune

A revolutionary perfume collection where fragrance meets fortune-telling. Each bottle is an interactive piece of art that combines luxury perfumery with elements of divination, creating a unique sensory experience that engages both scent and destiny

DARK ORACLE

Notes: Dark Vanilla, Black Orchid, Amber

A mysterious, deep fragrance featuring a rotating crystal chamber that reveals different fortunes through its viewing window

CELESTIAL DICE

Notes: Starflower, Moonstone, Silver Sage

An ethereal fragrance where the dice cap becomes a tool of divination. Each roll reveals a different fortune through the combination of mystical symbols

WHITE PROPHECY

Notes: Bergamot, White Lotus, Crystal Musk

A pure, illuminating fragrance that comes with heat-reactive fortune cards. As the candle warms the card, your prophecy is revealed through thermochromic ink
